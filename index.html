Double-Washed Sand

The preparation of sand consists of five basic processes which are natural decomposition, extraction, sorting, washing, and in some cases crushing. Sharp sand is mixed with concrete for a wide range of construction projects. Using sharp sand in concrete provides more strength and less flexibility compared to other types of sand. Sand is used to provide bulk, strength, and other properties to construction materials like asphalt and concrete.
